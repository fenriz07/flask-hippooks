Welcome to Hipcooks! Our philosophy is all about putting the fun back into food and getting you in touch with your inner chef. We love to cook without recipes or inhibitions, and we love to teach you mad skills! Of course, we share recipes after class, but we love it when you learn to wing it! In your own kitchens, you can be as exacting or as free-form as you like. Why use measuring cups when you can use your hands instead? They say that when food is delicious, it is because the person preparing it has sweet hands.

Always taste along the way, and insert your own personality into the food. Have fun with the process. More spicy? Go for it! A swirl of cream? Absolutely!

Our motto: cooking is always yummiest when it comes from the heart.
